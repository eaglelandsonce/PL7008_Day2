PRICING COMMITTEE GUIDANCE NOTE 2026-01 (FICTIONAL TRAINING DATA)
Single-transaction referral thresholds and triage rules

1. Premium size: Transactions above GBP 750m require Board notification before
   indicative pricing. Below GBP 750m, Pricing Committee approval suffices.
2. Data quality: Intake data quality score below 70/100 requires Pricing Committee
   escalation and a documented data remediation plan before quoting.
3. Deferred exposure: Transactions with deferred liabilities above 50% by value
   require explicit risk appetite sign-off.
4. Counterparty screening: Any counterparty (ceding insurer, trustee, sponsor,
   adviser) appearing on the internal watchlist as "review required" must be
   cleared by Compliance before a quote is released. "Internal caution list"
   entries require a documented mitigation note but do not block quoting.
5. Exclusivity: No exclusivity is granted before indicative pricing without
   Pricing Committee approval.
6. House longevity basis: CMI 2024 projections, 1.25% long-term improvement rate.
   Deviations from adviser bases must be quantified in the triage memo.
