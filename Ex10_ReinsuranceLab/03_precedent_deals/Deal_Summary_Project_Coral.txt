DEAL SUMMARY - PROJECT CORAL (FICTIONAL TRAINING DATA)
Status: Executed - August 2025
Transaction type: Longevity swap converted to buy-in, GBP 520m premium
Ceding insurer: Different UK ceding insurer (fictional: Harrogate Mutual)
Scheme profile: Manufacturing sector, 3,400 members, 58% pensioner liabilities
Benefit structure: RPI-linked capped at 5%, 50% spouse's pension

Outcome: Won. Closest analogue to a mid-size UK manufacturing scheme in the book.

Key lessons and sticking points:
1. Manufacturing sector schemes in our book have shown slightly heavier mortality
   than S3PA base tables suggest (sector adjustment of +2% applied to base mortality).
2. Trustees required a collateral account with weekly valuation - operationally
   demanding; ensure Ops signs off before final pricing.
3. Premium size around GBP 500m is squarely within single-transaction appetite;
   no Board referral was needed (threshold GBP 750m).

Counterparty notes: Sterling Rowe Advisory ran the process - well-organised,
strict on deadlines, penalised one reinsurer for a late round-one submission.
